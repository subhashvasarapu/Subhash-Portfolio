// about

From packets to parameters

I've spent my career deep in Azure networking — designing hub-spoke topologies, untangling ExpressRoute problems, writing KQL against terabytes of flow logs, and supporting enterprise customers when production is on fire.

That work taught me the things AI engineering actually needs: distributed systems intuition, production discipline, root-cause thinking, and infrastructure-as-code habits. Modern AI platforms are cloud systems with models in the middle — and I already speak the cloud half fluently.

I'm now on a structured 12-month journey into applied AI: PyTorch fundamentals, production RAG, AI agents, fine-tuning, and LLMOps — all built and deployed in public, on Azure. Every project below ships with IaC, CI/CD, and an evaluation suite, because "it demos well" isn't the same as "it's production-quality."
